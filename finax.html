Finax is a company aiming to be the most value adding and flexible cereal products company in the Nordic market. In this projects, I have tried to give a new allure to Finax Company and rebranded the Logo as well as the package of one of the most purchased products from Finax, which is flour. I wanted this product to stand out amongst its competition and to clearly be understood as an “All purpose flour” product.
