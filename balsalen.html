Balsalen Store

Work About Contact

Balsalen is a store of artefacts and design objects, located in the center of Aarhus, in Denmark that me and my colleagues chose to cooperate with. Our main goal for this project was to analyse the real problems the store is facing and design the best solution for them. Based on our thorough research we decided to focus on one target audience, which is women of middle age that like home decor and unique design pieces, and designed our solution based on a persona that matches these criteria. Our solution for Balsalen store covers such areas as branding and web development and results in a fully responsive and functional web platform that allows our persona to visualise the products, purchase them online, find more information about the store and its location and contact it via different platforms.
