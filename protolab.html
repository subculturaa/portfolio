Protolab is a student driven non-profit hub that started in 2017 in the Business Academy based in Aarhus. During its several years of activity, Protolab has been trying to reach students from all semesters to encourage socializing, networking and collaboration. Despite of all the positive aims and advantages for students, there has been noticed the lack of studentsâ€™ involvement and attendance in terms of workshops and social activities. In order to solve this problem, we came up with the brilliant idea of a Mobile Web App, that would allow students to log in, check the upcoming workshops in the calendar seaction, view the description, sign up for it and even suggest or create one event. We initialized our process by analyzing the audience and dividing it into groups that helped us further on to create Personas and identify their needs. Brainstorming, designing, testing, coding are some of the few activities we performed afterwards that resulted into a digital application meant to organize hub's events and expose the students to them. We have also initialized a set of strategies, involving business and advertizing techniques that would raise users' interest towards the product. We ended up having a product that is almost identical to the desired one and is being admired by the testers, which is the most important aspect.
